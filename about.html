This is the text for the first edition of my ABC book, A is for Anarchy.

It will likely first be physically published as a zine, though I'd like to eventually turn it into a board book. Phsyical publication is all the more important for an ABC book as I at least want my children living in the physical world before the digital one.

If you also wish to publish this, or modify it and create your own edition, by all means. That's the point. Please note the CC-BY-SA license.

It is intended that B and C, for example, be on pages such that if the book is open to B it is also open to C.
